Out-Patient Check-up Process and Flow Chart

Check-up Process

1. Get the queing number on the information table.

2. Wait for your queing number to be called.

3. Upon your number has called, go to the nurse station for getting up your record or if new patient, fill up your new record.

4. Pay in the cashier for checkup fee. If there is a health card wait for processing.

5. Get the doctors queing number.

6. Go to the doctors clinic and wait for the number to be called.

7. Upon calling your number by the doctor that is the time you can know what is your medical condition.

8. After getting the final say of the doctor, get the medical prescription and medical certificate.

9. End Process.
